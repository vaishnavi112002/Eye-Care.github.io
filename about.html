Shri Ganpati Netralaya Hospital

Shri Ganpati Netralaya was started on 20th october 2002 which was shifted in our own premises at Datta Nagar, Opp Uma College, Pandharpur in 2009. People have come to trust our hospital over these years for the treatment of sight-threatening diseases like cataract, glaucoma, Lasik Surgery retina problems. We strive to provide the best possible treatment for all our patients. Therefore, we offer an all-inclusive suite of treatments well-suited to the concerned patient.
The hospital is well equipped with great ambiance, latest technology, with well trained staff by taking all precautions for corona pandemic with primary care like sanitizer, digital thermometer ,face shield ,by keeping safe distance providing a diagnosis, investigation , giving medical as well surgical treatment. The hospital is well upgraded with the latest compact intuitive system for cataract phaco surgery with foldable unifocal as well as multifocal lenses, also the latest refractive excimer technology, zeiss mel 80 excimer laser for precise vision to see the world free from spects.

Our vision is that everyone has access to and receives high quality eye care for the prevention and treatment of eye disease in order to optimise, preserve ,love and protect your eyes.

Educating and generating innovative technology to eye care needs of society, contributing and implementing ideas that improve and advance vision health of local and global communities.
