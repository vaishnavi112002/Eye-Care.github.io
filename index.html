Be Careful About Your Eye

Shri Ganpati Netralaya was started on 20th october 2002 which was shifted in our own premises at Datta Nagar, Opp Uma College, Pandharpur in 2009. People have come to trust our hospital over these years for the treatment of sight-threatening diseases like cataract, glaucoma, Lasik Surgery retina problems. We strive to provide the best possible treatment for all our patients. Therefore, we offer an all-inclusive suite of treatments well-suited to the concerned patient.

Docter Profile

Dr.Sunita Karande is an consultant ophthalmologist in pandharpur and has an experience of 18 + years in my field. After completing my undergraduate I did my diploma in ophthalmology from bombay University. I took experience for sics,phaco surgeries from nab hospital miraj and performed multiple surgeries of anterior segment like phaco, pterygium, lid surgeries also i took training at baroda for refractive surgeries. My Interests also include neuro ophthalmology, Contact Lens Clinic,Medical Retina, Diabetic eye care and lasers. I also performed multiple lasik, refractive surgeries etc. I took multiple charity camps and operated for cataract, pterygium, and chalazion.
